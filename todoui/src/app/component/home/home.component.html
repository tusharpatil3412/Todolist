Todo Manegment

A to-do list, often abbreviated as "todo," is a list of tasks or activities that need to be completed, typically organized in a systematic or prioritized manner. The purpose of a to-do list is to help individuals or teams keep track of what needs to be done, manage their workload, and ensure that important tasks are not overlooked.
